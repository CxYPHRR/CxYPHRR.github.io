About

est. 5 yrs
The Short Version "You give the idea, I write your story."

I'm a fiction writer, a ghostwriter and a scriptwriter working across different tropes of fiction: the kind of fiction that make readers sit with discomfort, obsession, and even unresolved questions. I also write sci-fi, and it's where some of my sharpest world-building happens.

Most projects come to me as an idea, a voice sample, or a half-finished manuscript. Whether I'm writing under a client's name or creating original fiction, my job is the same: deliver compelling stories in a voice that feels authentic, polished, and impossible to distinguish from the writer's own. With five years of professional fiction writing experience, I specialize in turning concepts into finished novels that readers can't put down.

Outside of writing, I'm building a strong foundation in web design and front-end development. I use those skills to create and maintain this website, develop tools that streamline my writing process, and continually expand my JavaScript knowledge as I grow into a full front-end developer.
