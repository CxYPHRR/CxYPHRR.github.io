About

est. 5 yrs
the short version I write the books other names go on the cover of.

I'm a ghostwriter and scriptwriter working across dark romance, psychological horror, and audio drama: the kind of fiction that asks readers to sit with discomfort, obsession, and things that don't resolve cleanly. I also write sci-fi, and it's where some of my sharpest world-building happens.

Most projects come to me as an idea, a voice sample, or a half-finished draft. My job is to disappear into the client's voice and deliver chapters that read like they were always theirs — on schedule, without the seams showing.

Outside of client work, I build the tools I use to manage it: this site, my writing trackers, and the front-end skills that keep the business side of ghostwriting running without friction.
